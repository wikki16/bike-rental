About VelocityRide

VelocityRide is revolutionizing urban mobility with our premium bike rental service. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a single location to a city-wide network of rental stations, serving thousands of happy customers.

Our mission is to provide affordable, eco-friendly transportation options while promoting health and reducing urban congestion. All our bikes are regularly maintained and serviced to ensure your safety and comfort.
